About us

In 2005 a burning passion for movies sparked a friendship between the two cinema-lovers John Smith and Catharina Erkens. Smith worked part time as an employee at a local diner, and Erkens was a developer working at Microsoft. Due to the rapid growth of digital media, as well as the constant changes to physical media, the two wanted to create a catalogue of all their favorite movies, without having to occupy their living rooms with hundreds of cassettes and DVD's
